      So it’s 1:32 AM, and in my languid state, I have sunk into tumblr-dom. I’ve toyed with the idea for several months; initially to pass time during my final semester at IMSA, later to aid in the abandonment of Facebook-mania, and more recently in hopes of documenting my June trip to India with some modicum of orderliness. What stopped me all of those times was “that little voice in my head”, as some unwittingly describe it. I say ‘unwittingly’ because that voice is far from little. Sure, it’s invisible. But it’s one of the loudest voices that plagues the human mind; worse than parents that never stop lecturing, worse than annoying acquaintances that you only pretend are friends, even worse than Justin Bieber’s pre-pubescent shrills. The “little voice in your head” is the one that never shuts up, the one that tells you you aren’t good enough, the one that stops you from trying new things. With a lot of effort, one can diminish that voice until it’s small enough to be considered little. Until I reach that point, I’ll pick a different adjective

      But I digress. Point is, while I am confident in both my writing and my opinions, I’ve always been told (by that gargantuan voice) that I just don’t have the artistic ability necessary to maintain a tumblr. I still don’t believe I have that ability. Hopefully this website, with all its mystical powers, can prove me wrong.
